INGREDIENTS

The formula that gives you wiiings

Caffeine

This stimulant targets the central nervous system to enhance alertness and reduce the sensation of fatigue.

Taurine

This naturally occurring amino acid supports various metabolic processes and is a key functional component of the formula.

B-group vitamins

These essential micronutrients are vital for maintaining normal energy-yielding metabolism and reducing tiredness.

Sugars

Derived from sugar beets, these carbohydrates provide the body with a quick and accessible source of energy.

Water

Fresh Alpine water serves as the high-quality liquid foundation that carries all the other ingredients.
